Case study

Limarko.com

Website for one of the biggest shipping and logistic companies in the Baltic States

↓

Beginning


When we first met the Limarko president, Vytautas Lygnugaris, he wanted to improve and optimize his recent website for a better reach with his clients. So he contacted us to support him with our advice and expertise. He asked for a complete redesign, establishing a professional B2B online presence. And yes, we were ready and happy to help. The initial challenge was to rethink the existing content: Dropping the concept of only presenting the Limarko companies to instead showcasing all the services that they perform. Why? Because potential clients who visit their site want to know about services firsthand, they want to know what Limarko can do for them and what projects they already did for other clients.

Vytautas Lygnugaris

Limarko President & CEO

It is a pleasure to work with Kai and his team. They immediately understood our needs and created an excellent web experience for our company.

What we achieved


The “redesign” of the website was a complete new build, creating a unique web design from scratch and providing a new content model.

The new design contains beautiful graphical elements supporting the entire look and feel of the website. Inviting the visitors to find out more about Limarko and improving their user experience. Which gives potential and existing clients a better feeling of what they will actually experience from the company's services.

The site became multilingual supporting English, Lithuanian and Russian languages for main target audiences fo the company.

All content from old website was migrated, tested, improved, and arranged into new information structure.

We adapted the website for mobile devices. The new responsive website now displays information comfortably in smartphones, tablets, laptops or high resolution screens.

The Limarko management got a separate page where visitors and clients can see the main decision-makers behind Limarko.

A section for business news and project gallery was created showing what projects Limarko was doing recently as well as giving the company's website more boost for their online positioning (SEO).

We used a modern technique to play a beautiful video in a background of main page to immerse visitors and help understand services and scale of the company faster. The video features nice drone footage of Limarko facilities and services in action.

Besides the brand-new website architecture, we also developed implemented a back-end solution (also called a content management system or CMS) for Limarko. The CMS allows Limarko to easily manage the content on their website themselves.

Furthermore, we created an eye-catching, responsive mobile version of the website. Their new mobile enabled website is a plus for their potential and existing customers' web experience as more and more visitors are using mobile devices globally. The new Limarko website adapts to smartphones, tablets, laptops and high resolution screens with ease increasing usability for visitors who are on the go and needs to find information fast. It is also an advantage for Limarko's positioning on search engines too since Google ranks mobile-friendly websites higher nowadays

Meeting the deadline


Usually, business websites are developed within 3 - 6 months or even more depending on the size of the company, its internal structure, stakeholders, their services and the features necessary to reach the goals set up at the beginning of the project. With my team we evaluated the time needed to respect the quality, the goals and deadline required for this project. Since all members of our team have 10+ years of experience which makes them the right people to make the "impossible" possible we manage to find the right scheduling to meet quality and deadline with a little bit of extra time to iron the details if needed.

It was a nice challenge and we succeeded in time, we exactly finished the website at the day agreed upon. And even better: The entire Limarko team was extremely delighted about the outcome.

Final Results


Our goal was that all visitors of Limarko’s new website can easily find the information they need, providing an intuitive interface, following the basic KISS principle - most systems work best if they are kept simple rather than made complicated. The new look and feel of the website is supporting the entire simplicity and making the site usable for desktop and mobile users.

The Limarko board was satisfied with both final result and the process and we reached their expectations.
